<h1>About UCLA Holdings</h1>
<br>

The Ainu-related collections at UCLA are spread across three different venues: the <a href="https://www.library.ucla.edu/visit/locations/research-library/">Charles E. Young Research Library</a>, <a href="https://www.library.ucla.edu/visit/locations/library-special-collections">UCLA Library Special Collections</a>, and the <a href="https://fowler.ucla.edu/">Fowler Museum at UCLA</a>. Below we link to a compilation of holding information, including titles, authors, dates, institutional information pages, and more for the collection items included on this website. A selection of the early modern materials are also available digitized, links to which are included here.
